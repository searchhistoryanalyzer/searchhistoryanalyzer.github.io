Data for Analysis

Upload your ChatGPT conversation history* and see what topics you discuss more often, and how it evolves.

To extract your conversation history:

  1. Click on your account icon in the ChatGPT web app, and select Settings.
  2. Activate Data controls tab.
  3. Press Export.
  4. Extract conversations.json file from the archive that you obtained.
