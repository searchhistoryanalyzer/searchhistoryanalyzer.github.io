Input Data

This service lets you make your “mental portrait” by analyzing the history of your google searches over the years.

All data are processed locally - you never share it to our servers. Also you’ll be able to delete any undesired words from the results of analysis. But first, you need to complete a bit tricky procedure of retrieving the input data from Google.

To obtain the data, visit Google Takout page, select the “Search History” checkbox (and uncheck others), then download and unzip your archive.
