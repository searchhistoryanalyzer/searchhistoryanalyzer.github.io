Input Data

This service tries to create your “mental portrait” by analyzing the history of your google searches that have been accumulated over the years.

All data is processed locally in your browser*, and you never share it to our servers.

Also you’ll be able to delete any undesired words from the results of the analysis.

But first, you need to complete (a bit tricky) procedure of retrieving the input data from Google.

To obtain the data, visit Google Takout page, select the “Search History” checkbox (and unselect others), then download and unzip your archive.

To begin analysis, drag and drop desired JSON files from “Search History” folder to this window
